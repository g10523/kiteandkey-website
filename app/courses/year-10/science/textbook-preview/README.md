# Year 10 Science Textbook Preview Images

This folder contains the textbook preview images for Year 10 Science.

## Image Files

Place your textbook page images here with the following naming convention:

- `page1.jpg`
- `page2.jpg`
- `page3.jpg`
- `page4.jpg`
- `page5.jpg`
- etc.

The TextbookPreview component will automatically load and display these images in the carousel.

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Naming**: Sequential numbering starting from `page1.jpg`
- **Recommended size**: 1200px width for optimal quality
- **Aspect ratio**: Standard textbook page proportions

## Usage

The images will be displayed in the textbook preview section at the bottom of the Year 10 Science course page.
